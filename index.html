About Vick

Victor A Manjarrez was born on July 17th, 1993 in Chicago, IL. The son of Mexican immigrants, Vick grew up in the Humboldt Park neighborhood where he attended high school at Pritzker College Prep. At Pritzker, Vick excelled in academics and sports; he was a multiple time Honors student and was one of the star players in the Rugby team. After graduating from Pritzker, Vick went on to attend two years at Triton College and then another two years at UIC, where he received his BA in Economics. Shortly after graduating, Vick was hired by Chubb to work as a Risk Management analyst. Now, looking to obtain a whole new skillset, Vick has taken the challenge of attending Northwestern's Programming Boot Camp course in hopes of obtaining a career in Tech as a Software Engineer.

When he's not busy with work or stressing out with his Programming course, Vick enjoys training Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu and Muay Thai, photography, improving his Portuguese, and spending time with friends and family.
